Learning Outcome

From this blog, We will be able to learn a lot from the above unnoticed characters in Mahabharata like how powerful is Barbarika so that Krishna had to make a plan to let him not to participate in battle, how wise and good hearted person was in the side of Kauravas (Yuyutsu), might of a a powerful warrior belong to the Yadava-Vrishni dynasty of Lord Krishna named Satyaki, how vikarana stood against the injustice done to a woman and how Ulupi relieved the curses on Arjuna and showed her love towards him. These are some important life lessons we should know to follow and keep our loved ones safe. The Mahabharata, can teach to the world. With the same thought I went about researching for their stories. Most of them had highly provoking tales to be told, but it is sad that these characters were not given the needed limelight in the popular epic. Their aspect of loyalty, their respect towards each other’s words, their wisdom and on top of all their lionhearted brave attitude teach us the how to inculcate required ethical lifestyle and stand on our feet for fighting against Adharma. Knowing these stories not only gave me a deeper insight about the popular Indian mythology but also gave me different viewpoints and opinions which I can use in my life for making crucial decisions.
